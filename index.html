BLACK PINK

Wolrd Tour 'Born Pink'

We have the honour of announcing that the Wolrd Tour 'Born Pink' is coming soon. Taking place from October 2022 and ending in June 2023, this will be "the most epic world tour in the history of K-pop girl groups." Born Pink is also the group's third tour, after BLACKPINK Arena Tour in Japan in 2018 and In Your Area globally in 2018-2020. The first show was dedicated to "Korean home ground" Seoul. Immediately after, the girls will fly directly to the US for a series of shows in October and November. Then Europe from November and December. BLACKPINK's tour returns to Asia in early 2023, and ends in Australia summer 2023. We are currently selling tickets for the first three locations.
